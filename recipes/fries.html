French Fries

Credit: Recipe adapted from allrecipes.com

Homemade seasoned French Fires to beat the drive-thru!

A simple recipe to make seasoned french fries. These fries are easy to make at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 & 1/2 pounds of potatoes(peeled)
  • 1 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes and place them in cold water
  2. Get oil and heat it to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C) on a pan or skillet
  3. In the meantime, place flour,garlic salt, onion salt, paprika, and salt into a bowl. Proceed to stir water as to construct a thick batter.
  4. Drain fries and pat dry to then dip each fry into the batter.
  5. Drop fries into the oil individually and fry for 7-10 minutes to ensure the gold and crispy look.
  6. Finally, drain fries on a paper towel and enjoy!
