Chocolate Chip Cookies

Credit: Recipe adapted from allrecipes.com

Cookies that are easy to make at home! Fresh from the oven!

Classic Chocolate Cookie recipe containing simple ingredients. Great recipe to follow when in a crave for Chocolate chip cookies!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of instant espresso coffee powder
  • 1 1/2 cups of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F or 190 degrees C. Prep baking sheet by greasing it.
  2. In bowl, mix the butter, brown sugar and white sugar until a smooth texture appears.
  3. Beat the egg and vanilla into the bowl.
  4. Sift in the flour, baking soda, baking powder, salt and espress powder.
  5. Stir into mixture and stir in the choclate chips.
  6. Place dough through tablespoonfuls on the baking sheet. Allow for 1 & 1/2 to 2 inches in between each cookie. Expect between a dozen or 15 cookies.
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes and remove from baking sheets to cool on wire racks. Enjoy!
