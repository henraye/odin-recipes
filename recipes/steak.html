Flat Iron Steak

Credit: Recipe adapted from allrecipes.com

A flat iron steak recipe that is easy to follow!

The perfect flat iron steak to make. Following the recipe will allow you to make high quality steak at home that rivals restaurants themselves!

Ingredients

  • 2 pound flat iron steak
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 teaspoon of chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon of chopped fresh chives
  • 1/4 cup of dry wine
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of dry mustard powder

Steps

  1. Have the steak inside a resealable bag. Stir together the olive oil, garlic, parsley, rosemary, chives, red wine, salt, pepper, and mustard powder in a bowl.
  2. Marinade the steak in bag and remove as much air as possible to then allow steak to be in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours.
  3. Using a non-stick pan, have the heat set to medium-high. Cook the steak for 3 to 4 minutes on each side for medium rare, longer for desired doneness.
  4. Let steak rest for 5 minutes to allow it to soak in the seasonings.
